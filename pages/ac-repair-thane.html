Common AC Problems We Fix

Our technicians are trained to diagnose and resolve a wide range of AC issues. Some of the most common problems we encounter include:

  • AC Not Cooling: This could be due to low refrigerant levels, a dirty air filter, or a faulty compressor.
  • Water Leakage: Often caused by a clogged drain pipe or improper installation.
  • Strange Noises: Grinding or squealing sounds usually indicate a problem with the fan motor or belt.
  • AC Not Turning On: This could be an electrical issue, a blown fuse, or a faulty thermostat.
  • Bad Odor from AC: Usually caused by mold or bacteria growth inside the unit.
