Common Microwave Problems

  • Microwave Not Heating: Usually a magnetron or high-voltage diode failure.
  • Turntable Not Rotating: Often a faulty motor or broken roller guide.
  • Sparking Inside: Could be a damaged waveguide cover or metal inside.
  • Buttons Not Working: Usually a control panel or touchpad issue.
  • Microwave Shuts Off: Often due to overheating or a faulty door switch.
