Common Washing Machine Issues

  • Machine Not Starting: Could be a door lock issue or PCB failure.
  • Water Not Draining: Often due to a clogged pump or drain hose.
  • Vibrating Excessively: Usually caused by unbalanced loads or worn-out shock absorbers.
  • Leaking Water: Could be a damaged door seal or hose.
  • Not Spinning: Often a motor or belt problem.
