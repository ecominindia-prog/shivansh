Common Gas Stove Problems

  • Gas Leakage: A critical issue that needs immediate attention.
  • Burner Not Lighting: Often due to a clogged nozzle or faulty igniter.
  • Low Flame: Usually caused by a blockage in the gas pipe or burner.
  • Yellow Flame: Indicates improper gas-to-air ratio or dirty burners.
  • Knob Stuck: Mechanical issue with the gas valve.
